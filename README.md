# School Data Analysis
## Project Objective
Read, transform and manipulate the data from the school data CSV and find key factors as to why certain schools perform better than others with math and reading scores.
## Tools
Python Pandas Libary
## Key Insights
- 8 performing schools they are charter schools.
- The top 8 performing schools are of the size small-medium except for Pena High school.
- Small and medium schools greatly out perform large schools with small schools overall passing at 89.88 and medium schools at 90.62. Large schools with an overall passing at 58.28
